Welcome

The dynamic and innovative field of Computer Science! We are thrilled to have you join our community of aspiring programmers, problem solvers, and tech enthusiasts.As you embark on this exciting journey, you'll discover that Computer Science is not just about coding; it's a gateway to unlocking endless possibilities and shaping the future. Whether you're passionate about software development, artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, or any other area within the realm of computing, you'll find a wealth of opportunities to explore and contribute.Our department is committed to providing you with a stimulating learning environment, cutting-edge resources, and a supportive community of faculty, staff, and fellow students.

Our Organization

The University of Ghana is one of the Africa's most preeminent public universities. Our impact on individuals, our region and the world is profound. whether we are launching young people into a boundless future or confronting the grand challenges of our time through dauntless research. UG educates more than 38,000students annually, with approximately 2.64% being computer science students. We turn ideas into impact and transform the world around us. For more about our impact, visit our news site, UG News.
