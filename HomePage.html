What is Computer Science?

Computer science is a multidisciplinary field that encompasses the study of computation, algorithms, data structures, programming languages, software development, artificial intelligence, and the theoretical foundations of computing. It involves the design, analysis, and implementation of computer systems and software to solve complex problems. Computer science has a profound impact on various aspects of modern life, influencing technology, business, communication, and scientific research. Key areas within computer science include software engineering, computer networks, databases, machine learning, and cybersecurity. As technology continues to advance, computer science plays a crucial role in shaping the digital landscape and driving innovation across industries.
